The goal of this website

Is inspired by a Hadith from Prophet Muhammad (Peace Be Upon Him)

Abu Hurairah (May Allah be pleased with him) reported:
The Messenger of Allah (ﷺ) said, "When a man dies, his deeds come to an end except for three things:
Ongoing Charity (Sadaqah Jariyah), Beneficial Knowledge and a virtuous descendant who prays for him (for the deceased).
Sahih Muslim 1631
  1. Ongoing Charity: I'm not yet sure how this website will achieve this goal, but InshAllah God will reveal this to me at the appointed time.
  2. Beneficial Knowledge: Sharing useful information that people may find helpful.
  3. A virtuous descendant who prays for him: If God blesses me with offspring. I would love for this to be a reference they can use to remember me and keep me in their duas (prayers).



Formerly Known as Tomiwa Ademidun

That was my name before I became Muslim. Some people still call me that.

Becoming Muslim has been such a profound change on my life that I am gradually transitioning to a new identity to reflect externally the change I feel spiritually.

Rather than delete or downplay my past identity prior to Islam, I look at it as a digital archive of the man I used to be and the person I have turned into through the mercy of God.
