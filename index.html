Surah Fussilat 41:33

My name is Abd Al-Nasr. I am a Muslim. I enjoy worshipping God, reading Qur'an, doing Dawah (inviting others to Islam), spending time with family and friends, reading, writing, farming, cooking and cleaning.

Previously Known as Tomiwa Ademidun

Before I becameI became Muslim, I went by Tomiwa Ademidun. Becoming Muslim had such a profound change on my life that I am gradually in the process of transitioning to a new identity to reflect externally the change I feel spiritually. However, rather than delete my identity in my period of Jahiliya (ignorance) prior to Islam can be a useful guide for people to see my journey and for me to reflect and help myself and those around me acclamatize to this new change.
